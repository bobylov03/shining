Meet Anna Bobylyova

The Artist Behind Shining Strings

Shining Strings is the artistic creation of Anna Bobylyova, a virtuoso violinist with over two decades of international performance experience. Originally from Ukraine and now based in the wonderful Muskoka, Canada, Anna brings a unique blend of European classical training and global artistic sensibility to every performance.

Having graced stages from the grand theaters of Europe to luxury cruise vessels, Anna has cultivated a performance style that is both technically impeccable and emotionally resonant. This journey across continents—performing in Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and the Americas—has refined an ability to connect with diverse audiences and adapt to any event atmosphere.

Each Shining Strings performance is more than background music; it's a curated emotional journey that transforms events into unforgettable experiences. Whether it's the tender vulnerability of a wedding processional or the sophisticated ambiance of a corporate gala, the music is thoughtfully tailored to elevate your special moment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

Booking 4-8 months in advance is highly recommended for all events. Popular dates (especially summer weekends) often book up even earlier.

Do you provide your own equipment?

Yes, I bring a professional sound system suitable for venues of up to 300 guests. For larger events, additional sound equipment can be arranged.

Can you learn specific songs for my event?

Absolutely! Custom arrangements are one of my specialties. Please provide song requests at least 4 weeks before your event to allow time for preparation.

What is your cancellation policy?

A 50% deposit is required to secure your date. Cancellations made more than 90 days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 90 days are subject to the deposit being retained.

Do you travel outside of Muskoka?
